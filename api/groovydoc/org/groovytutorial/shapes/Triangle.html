      • Constructor Detail

        • Triangle(java.lang.Number a, java.lang.Number b, java.lang.Number c)

          Configures the sides (a, b, c) of the triangle and calls the perimeter property of the Sides trait in order to make the sides (mostly) immutable and lock in the perimeter calculation The protected calculateArea method is called to determine the area of the triangle. The result is assigned to the area field.

          throws:
          IllegalArgumentException if one of the sides <= 0
          Parameters:
          a - One of the triangle's three sides
          b - One of the triangle's three sides
          c - One of the triangle's three sides
